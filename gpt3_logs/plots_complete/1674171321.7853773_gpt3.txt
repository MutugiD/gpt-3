The police are under immense pressure to find the killer. They bring in the best detectives from all over the country to try to solve the case. However, the killer always seems to be one step ahead of them.

The film culminates in a tense showdown between the detectives and the killer. The detectives finally catch the killer and put an end to his reign of terror.
