But Aisha is determined to fight on, inspired by the hope of freedom for her people. against all odds, she leads her ragtag group of rebels in a series of daring attacks against the British.

But the British are relentless in their pursuit, and soon Aisha and her group are surrounded. Outnumbered and outgunned, they make their final stand.
