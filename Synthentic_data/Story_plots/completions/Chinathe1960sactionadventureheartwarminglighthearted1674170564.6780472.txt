When American accountant John arrives in China to help set up a new office for his company, he quickly realizes that things are very different than he expected. The people are friendly, but the language and cultural barriers are daunting. With the help of his new assistant, Mei, John starts to acclimate to his new surroundings.

Mei is a vibrant young woman who is excited to help John learn about her culture and to show him the beauty of her country. She takes him on a tour of the Great Wall, and they explore the Forbidden City and other historical landmarks. She also introduces him to traditional Chinese food and medicine.

As John and Mei get to know each other better, they start to develop feelings for each other. They share a romantic dinner together, and John finally musters up the courage to kiss her.

However, just as things are heating up between them, the Cultural Revolution begins, and Mei is forced to flee the country. John is heartbroken, but he knows that he must respect her decision and watch from afar as she fights for her country's freedom.
